Defaulter Notification System

Rhytmelo Designs is excited to introduce a new feature to help manage overdue payments. This system will automatically monitor payment statuses, ensuring seamless and transparent payment processes for your business.

From the due date up to 100 days, a warning will appear in the browser console for any overdue payments. Beyond 100 days, a banner will be displayed on your website as a persistent reminder to settle the dues.
